Blue Force's catastrophic fortune (roll: 5) manifests as a single, specific event on the main axis: as Shadow Recon begins to move out of F4 toward E5, a lead vehicle suffers a catastrophic mechanical failure before the column fully departs F4. Shadow at F4 is at the eastern edge of the suppression zone but not directly in it; the fires cause disruption and keep Shadow pinned at F4 but do not inflict direct casualties.

Thunder Battery's fire mission against Red Guard at C5 is assessed independently of the highway fortune event. The battery executes its mission from L7 against the light urban target at C5. Range (~10km) and entrenchment (30%) degrade effectiveness, and without a confirmed forward observer at or near C5, the battery relies on map registration. The mission achieves moderate suppression — Red Guard is disrupted and cannot improve its entrenchment this turn, but is not significantly attrited. Red Guard holds its positions. Alpha Company advances cleanly to F5 (Ashbury) on a parallel axis unaffected by the F4 congestion. Bravo Company advances to G6 on the southern axis without incident. By end of turn, Blue's main axis is stalled at F4/G4 (Steel disordered, Shadow broken down), with Alpha at F5 as the westernmost functional Blue element. The bridge at D5 remains intact. Red's defensive line is fully intact with Viper now repositioned at C4 covering the northern approaches.
